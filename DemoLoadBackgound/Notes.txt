Setting up the SD card on the Emulator
1. Create new ADV with SD card (set the size)
2. Launch the emulator
3. In eclipse switch to DDMS view
4. Choose the emulator on the left and oprn the "File Explorer" tab
5. Navigate to mnt/sdcard/pictures
6. Click on the icon with the arrow to the device (top right corner)
7. Choose the file you want uploaded to the device
8. Restart the emulator.
9. In the emulator go to the gallery - the picture should be there

Enjoy :)
